Rules

You can manganize your pictures here, if the following rules are met: Only style of commissions I'm currently open to. Send a single person photo reference and I'll send you back a the picture when a spot becomes available. You can pay me whatever price you think my work was worth to you. Similar commission work typically costs around EURO 15. Type of emails/photos that will get ignored/trashed:
  • Missing a photo
  • Not including "I want this in Anime style" or "I want this in Chibi style"
  • Deadlines ("Must be done for a birthday, anniversary, live event...")
  • Photos of Couples or Groups
  • Extreme close ups
  • NSFL(Not Safe For Life) photos
  • Blurry, small, bland, or complicated photos
  • Person interacting with medium to large objects
  • Person interacting with other people
  • More than one pet
  • Art Trades
  • Not relating at all to portraits
  • Offers to work on your commercial projects
Please view my pencil portrait gallery below to give you a general idea what I will accepted. If you like, you can send your request along with your picture to me at: Sorry for the all the rules/litmus test. It simply makes my life a whole lot easier to focus on drawing for my customers and not having to email people back and forth all day with these details. If you did follow everything, don't be offended if I don't get back to you! I may be with other customers, the photo just doesn't inspire me, working on other bigger projects, family stuff... If you like the service to continue or you like the pictures that you see on my page, then you can make donations via paypal or tell all your friends about this site to get the service going. Because drawing materials, server and domain costs have to be paid. I'd appreciate. Thank you in advance.
